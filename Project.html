PROJECTS

Raven

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

RAVEN, is an indigenously developed VTOL (Vertical Take-off and Landing) capable fixed-winged aircraft that can transition from vertical flight to forward flight. It is primarily a delivery drone that can also be used as a surveillance drone. Because of its VTOL capability, it can operate in the remotest of places without the need for a runway.The provision of basic supplies is hampered in natural disaster. Essential supplies can be airdropped through this without much stress about the take off and landing.

Reaper

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

Reaper is our surveillance UAV drone, capable of sustained flight, which was inspired by the General Atomics MQ-9 Reaper. It is a cost-effective solution for a reliable and high-endurance UAV for various outdoor operations.The UAV is designed to obtain a high glide ratio to maximize the operational range to prevent it from crashing in case of equipment failure.This MALE UAV system meet the demands of industries that require surveillance, terrain mapping, small cargo delivery, and search operations during disasters.

Agrodrone

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

An autonomous agricultural drone capable of smart seeding, spraying pesticide as well as mapping of fields and using soil gradients to identify moisture content in fields.The drone through various sensors takes data input and decides whether and where to spray. If spraying is needed a relay works, which turn on the motor and spraying in on the area required. Agro-drones have the potential to revolutionize agriculture by increasing efficiency, reducing costs, improving crop yields, enhancing sustainability, and increasing safety.

Human Following drone

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

Human following Drone is an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) that uses advanced sensors, computer vision algorithms and mapping techniques to track and follow a human subject.It is designed to be portable,easy to use,making it ideal for a wide range of applications ,including photography,videography,search and rescue and survelliance.

Ornithopter

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

The ornithopter is one of the most beautiful-looking projects of the club. It is a Bird-like flying winged Aircraft with the ability to mimic their flight characteristic. This project focuses on studying and applying different mechanisms to convert rotary motion into linear material used for the wings that could achieve improved performance.

RedFalcon

Team Members : Swapn,Vedant,Ani,Stuti,Rajat

Red Falcon is an RC aircraft that can operate from short runways to carry outsized cargo as well as regular cargo.Payload will consist of large spherical storage containers and Regular Boxed Cargo which must be carried on each flight. Huge wing span and an innovative landing gear made up of PLA makes it even more effective.This aircraft can be used in natural disaster affected areas as well as difficult terrains where it is pretty difficult to reach.
